Among the affiliated educational institutions of Pokhara University, it is the first educational institution to receive the Quality Assurance and Accreditation (QAA) certificate from the University Grants Commission (UGC).

QAA Certificate and 24 Years of Experience

National Academy of Science and Technology, popularly known as NAST, is a QAA Certified, English-medium co-educational institution. Patroned and promoted by a team of academicians, researchers, and professionals renowned both at home and abroad, NAST has proved itself as a center of learning for academic excellence in the fields of science, technology, and management.
